About ClipsKit

ClipsKit sends out clippings of all relevant articles to one or multiple keywords over a specified window at designated times.

ClipsKit is currently in the beta testing phase and is available free of charge, with no expectation of future payment or use.

In its current form, ClipsKit can save 50-70% of the time typically spent on human-powered press clippings, depending on the individual use case. Download a sample below!
